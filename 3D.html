Food Series

In this project, I used Blender to create a realistic 3D donut from scratch. I focused on modeling, texturing, and lighting techniques to bring out the details, from the smooth dough to the glossy icing with sprinkles. This project helped me improve my understanding of Blender’s tools and workflows, especially in achieving proper lighting for realistic renders.
